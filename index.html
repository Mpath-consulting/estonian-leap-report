Mission thesis

Peace is more profitable than war. Nations that invest in digital infrastructure, talent development, and innovation systematically outperform peers locked in conflict and analog administration.

Estonia is a live-case blueprint of this thesis. This assembly is designed to export that model – aligning capital, policy, technology, and talent around a concrete leap from approximately €15B to €50B GDP by 2030.

What makes this gathering different

  • Curated ecosystem assembly – not a conference, not a panel format.

  • Estonia positioned as the operational hub of a New Nordics innovation corridor.
